#KudzayiJoinsTheBand!

Hey Spotify, I created this (less formal) page just for you. Here's the thing, the vibes at Spotify are immaculate, you guys can't just let anyone into the band! So I interviewed with myself to gather some answers to the important questions:

  • Do I have any real-life "band" experience?

    Yes! I was in a rap group called RCK (Rap Chronic Kings lol) back in high school. We only released one(1) EP, our peers loved it, I'd even call it "critically acclaimed."

  • Any podcasts that I listen to on Spotify?

    Yup, I usually listen to podcasts when I'm preparing dinner or on the treadmill. My favourites are Laker Film Room, Nintendo VoiceChat and The Mismatch with Kevin O'Connor and Chris Vernon.

  • Who's an artist I've discovered through Spotify?

    I feel like I discover a new artist every day on Spotify, that algorithm is incredible! I hope to get to write some code on it someday.... But to answer your question - Unknown Mortal Orchestra

  • I'm in a karaoke showdown, What's my go-to song?

    Candy Rain by Soul For Real.

  • Top 3 artists I would most like to see perform live?

    Wow, there are so many I could mention! But off the top of my head - Toro Y Moi, Frank Ocean, and Tinashe.
